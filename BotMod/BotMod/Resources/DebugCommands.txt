Debug Commands:
debug.ShowCommands - Toggles the debug commands menu.
debug.ModifyDifficulty - Used to access the difficulty menu.
debug.RandomLocations - Toggles random locations.
debug.MaxPower <integer> - Sets the max power to the integer given.
For max powers less than 100, use put 0s before the number i.e 099
Max here is 999. Max for main selection is 32-bit limit.
debug.LockDifficulty - Locks the difficulty menu.
debug.UnlockDifficulty - Unlocks the difficulty menu.
